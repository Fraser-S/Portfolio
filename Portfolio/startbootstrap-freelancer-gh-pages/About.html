Introduction

I started programming in 2007 and have been ever since. During the past 10 years, I have used a wide range of languages and API's at college and University that have helped me make several 2D and 3D games.

At College I studied Computer games Development where I spent my time learning how to use c++ and c# to make games using XNA and DarkGDK. Here I also learnt some basic 3D modeling techniques and had access to the Unreal Engine.

After College I went to University where I studies Computer Games Technology where I mostly used c++ to create games using OpenGL. Even though I was doing a games course I have also had done modules that covered machine learning and computer vision using OpenCV aswell as modules on GPGPU programming where I used CUDA and OpenCL to perform parallel processing.
